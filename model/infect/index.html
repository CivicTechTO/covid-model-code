Animated Covid Model/Game

Introduction

This is an animated illustration of how Covid-19 spreads. It is also a game that shows the effects of some public health interventions. The underlying model steps in time, and shows individual people. It displays the location and health status of each person.
It assumes large individual variations in infectiousness, and that distance, ventilation, and volume of breath are significant factors.
The game is played from the point of view of being in political charge of the town. The actions that you control are things designed to control the spread of the virus. The game is scored in political points. Events and actions have political costs. The object of the game is to keep the score as low as possible.
Exposition runs for an indefinite period. The game runs for 400 days.
