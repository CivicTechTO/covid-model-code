Animated Covid Model/Game

Description

  • Display
  • Controls
    • General Controls
      • Start Game
        • This stops the illustration and starts the game play.
      • Run/Pause
        • This toggles between running and paused.
      • Speed
        • Toggles between displaying results after every model step and displaying results after a number of model steps.
    • Game Controls
      • Mask level
        • This sets the probability for each person effectively wearing a mask.
      • Open/Close
        • Thes buttons toggle whether the indicated room type is open or closed.
        • Schools
        • Offices
        • Meat packing
        • Houses of worship
        • Restaurants
        • Bars
        • Clubs
        • Parks
        • House parties
  • Statistics
    • We display the current value, the maximum value, and the total value for the following numbers of people.
    • Well - People who have never been infected.
    • Infected - People who are infected.
    • Incubating - People who are infected but not yet infectious.
    • Infectious - People who are infectious.
    • Symptomatic - People who are showing symptoms.
    • Sick at home - People who are sick enough to stay home (includes those in hospital).
    • Needs hospital - People who need to go the hospital.
    • In hallway - People who are in the hospital hallways (may need ward or ICU).
    • Needs ICU - People who need the ICU.
    • Dead - People who have died.
  • Charts
    • The charts show the current value of the following values:
    • Symptomatic - People who are showing symptoms.
    • Sick at home - People who are sick enough to stay home (includes those in hospital).
    • Needs hospital - People who need to go the hospital.
    • In hallway - People who are in the hospital hallways (may need ward or ICU).
